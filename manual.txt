1. GETTING STARTED
═══════════════════

The map is a single file (index.html). No installation or internet
server is needed — just double-click to open it in any modern
browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).

Your notes, visits, and saved routes are stored on your own
computer using the browser's local storage. They persist even
after you close the page and come back later.

  ⚠ IMPORTANT: Clearing your browser history/cache will erase
     your notes and saved routes. Always export before clearing.


2. BROWSING BUSINESSES
══════════════════════

The map shows all 1,093 businesses as colored dots:

  Blue     = Government / Education / Library
  Green    = Grocery / Restaurant / Food / Convenience
  Red      = Auto / Auto Parts / Repair
  Purple   = Healthcare / Dental / Vet
  Orange   = Retail / Gift / Hardware
  Brown    = Lodging / Hotel / Motel
  Yellow   = Religion
  Gray     = Services / Other

Filtering:
┬───────────┬─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Region    │ Limits to one of the 5 regions              │
├───────────┼─────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Town      │ Limits to a specific town                   │
├───────────┼─────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Search    │ Type any part of a business name            │
└───────────┴─────────────────────────────────────────────┘

The count at the top shows how many businesses match your
current filters. The same filters apply to the map and the
Browse list.

Click any marker to see full details (address, phone, email,
website) in a popup.

The sidebar has three tabs:

  📋 Browse    — Sortable table of all businesses
  📍 Route     — Build and save driving routes
  📝 Notes     — View your notes and visit history


3. ROUTE PLANNING
═════════════════

Building a Route:
──────────────
  1. Click a business marker on the map
  2. It's added to the Route Planner sidebar
  3. Repeat for all stops
  4. Each stop shows a numbered marker on the map

Managing Stops:
─────────────
  • Reorder: Drag the ⠿ handle up or down
  • Remove: Click the ✕ button on a stop
  • Toggle: Click a marker a second time to remove it

Optimizing a Route:
────────────────
  Click "🗺 Optimize Route" to automatically reorder
  your stops in the most efficient driving order.
  This uses OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine) — a free
  online service. The route line draws in blue on the map.

  ⚠ OSRM requires internet access. If you're offline, the
     optimize feature won't work.

The route summary shows:
  • Total distance
  • Estimated driving time
  • Per-leg distances and times

Saving a Route:
────────────
  • Click "💾 Save Current"
  • Give it a name and click "Save"
  • The route appears in the "Saved Routes" list below

Loading a Saved Route:
───────────────────
  Click the route name in the "Saved Routes" list.

  Loading a route replaces your current route stops.

Deleting a Saved Route:
─────────────────────
  Click the ✖ button next to the route name.

  ⚠ This cannot be undone unless you have a backup export.

Printing a Route:
──────────────
  Click "🖨 Print" to print a route sheet for your trip.


4. NOTES & VISIT TRACKING
═════════════════════════

Adding a Note:
────────────
  1. Click a business marker to open its popup
  2. Type your note in the text box at the bottom
  3. Notes auto-save as you type — no save button needed

  Notes are useful for:
  • Recording conversations ("Spoke with owner Jane")
  • Tracking preferences ("Prefers email over phone")
  • Noting follow-up actions ("Call back Tuesday")

  Businesses with notes show a small red dot on their marker.

Editing a Note:
────────────
  Re-open the popup and edit the text. It saves automatically.

Deleting a Note:
─────────────
  Clear all the text and click away — the note is removed.

Marking a Business as Visited:
─────────────────────────────
  • Click "✅ Mark Visited" in the popup
  • The date is recorded
  • The marker shows a green checkmark

  • Click "Visit Again" to record another visit date
  • All visit dates are stored in your visit history

The Notes & Visits Tab:
─────────────────────
  Click the "📝 Notes & Visits" tab in the sidebar to see:

  • "All"        — All businesses that have any activity
  • "With Notes" — Only businesses that have notes
  • "Visited"    — Only businesses you've visited

  The list is sorted with most recently updated at the top.
  Each item shows:
  • Business name and location
  • Note content (if any)
  • Last visit date (if any)

  Click "📍 Show on Map" to fly to any business on the map.

  Click "⬇ Export" to download all your notes and visits
  as a JSON file for backup.

  Click "⬆ Import" to restore notes and visits from a
  previously exported file.


5. EXPORTING & BACKING UP YOUR DATA
════════════════════════════════════

All your notes, visits, and saved routes are stored in your
browser. To avoid losing them:

  → Export from the Notes tab:
    Click "⬇ Export" to download your notes + visits as
    a JSON file.

  → Export from the Route tab:
    Click "⬇ Export" under Saved Routes to download your
    saved routes as a separate JSON file.

  → To restore, click "⬆ Import" and select the file.

  → To transfer to another computer:
    1. Export both notes and saved routes on computer A
    2. Copy the JSON files to computer B
    3. Open index.html on computer B
    4. Import both files

  ⚠ The map itself (index.html) doesn't contain your
     personal data — only the business directory. Your
     notes and routes live in your browser's storage.


6. ROUTE PRINTING
═════════════════

When you click "🖨 Print", the route prints with:
  • A map showing the route line
  • A numbered table of stops with addresses

  Print styles are designed to be clean and ink-efficient.
  Background colors, buttons, and controls are hidden.
