Coffee News of Aroostook — Business Directory Map
User Manual
===================================================

WHAT THIS IS
------------
An interactive map of 1,141 businesses across Aroostook County, Maine,
sorted into 4 regions. Made for ad-sales field work — look up businesses,
narrow your search, write notes, mark when you visit, plan driving routes,
and make lists you can open in Excel or print.

Open the file called index.html to start. No other setup needed.

LOOKING UP BUSINESSES
---------------------
When you open the page, the map shows all 863 businesses that are good
candidates for ads. Colored dots on the map stand for different types
of businesses. The color key is in the bottom-right corner.

Tap any dot on the map to see a window with:
  • Business name, address, phone, email, website
  • Add or Remove from Route
  • Directions (opens Google Maps)
  • Share button
  • Mark as Visited


NARROWING YOUR SEARCH
---------------------
Use the controls at the top to shrink your results:

  Region ▼      — pick one of 4 areas
  Town ▼        — shows towns in the region you picked
  Category ▼    — pick from 7 types
  Search        — finds matches by name, address, town, type, or phone number
  🎤            — voice search (speak into your microphone)

Each option has a number in parentheses, like "Food (342)". That's how
many businesses fit that choice.

On a phone (held normally or sideways), the search controls hide behind
a 🔍 Filter button to save space. Tap it to see all the options. On a
phone held sideways, the list or notes panel appears on the right side
instead of covering the whole screen. The top bar hides after a few
seconds to show more of the map — tap anywhere on the screen to bring
it back.


BUSINESS DETAIL CARD
--------------------
Tap a dot on the map, or tap a row in the Browse list, to open the
detail card. It shows full contact info. Tap the 📋 buttons next to
an address or phone number to copy it.

From the detail card you can:
  • Get directions
  • Add or Remove from Route
  • Share with someone
  • Write a note
  • Set a follow-up date
  • Mark as visited
  • See nearby businesses (within half a mile)


NOTES
-----
You can write a note for any business. Notes are saved on your device
and stay there until you clear them.

Access notes from:
  1. Detail card — type your note and it saves automatically
  2. Notes & Visits panel

Each note can have a follow-up date (tap the calendar to pick one).
When you save the note, the date is saved with it.


NOTES & VISITS TAB (📝 Notes)
------------------------------
Inside the Notes tab, there are smaller tabs:

  All             — businesses that have a note OR a visit
  With Notes      — only businesses that have a note
  Visited         — only businesses you marked as visited
  + Add Note      — look up any business to add a note
  ⏰ Follow-ups   — see upcoming callbacks grouped by urgency

Each business in the list has:
  • Follow-up: <calendar> — pick a date to call them back
  • A box to write your note
  • Save / Delete buttons
  • 🗺 Show on Map — centers the map on this business
  • ✕ Remove — deletes the last visit date

FOLLOW-UPS ⏰
-------------
The Follow-ups tab groups businesses by how soon their date is:

  🔴 Overdue (N)     — dates that have already passed (red labels)
  🟠 Due Today (N)   — dates that are today (orange labels)
  🔵 Coming Up (N)   — dates within the next 3 days (blue labels)
  📅 Later (N)       — dates more than 3 days away

Within each group, the earliest dates come first.


VISITS
------
You can mark a business as visited from its map window or detail card.
Each visit gets a date stamp. Visits show a green ✅ label in the
Notes tab.

To remove a visit: tap ✕ Remove next to the date.


ROUTE PLANNER (📍 Route)
-----------------------
1. Pick a preloaded route from the 📋 Preloaded Routes section,
   or tap dots on the map to add your own stops
2. Stops appear in the Route tab — each stop shows its distance
   from the previous stop (straight-line estimate by default, or
   actual driving distance after tapping 🗺 Optimize)
3. Preloaded routes load the first 15 stops into your active route.
   The rest wait in the queue. As you mark visits or remove stops,
   the route automatically refills from the queue — you never see
   a huge list, and Google/Apple Maps always gets a manageable number
4. Slide the ⠿ handle up or down to change the order
5. Tap 🗺 Optimize to reorder stops for the shortest drive
6. Tap 📍 Navigate to get turn-by-turn directions on your phone
   (Apple Maps on iPhone, Google Maps on Android)
   — only the first 12 stops are sent since that's all maps can handle
7. Tap 🖨 Print for a paper copy of the route
8. Tap ✕ Clear to remove all stops and empty the queue


PRELOADED ROUTES
-----------------
The Route tab has a "Preloaded Routes" section with routes ready to go:
  • One route per town — tap a town name to load every suitable
    business, sorted by distance from town center (closest first)
  • Corridor routes — multi-town trips like "Presque Isle to Caribou"
  • First 15 stops go into your active route; the rest wait in the
    queue (shown as "Queue: N remaining" in the route summary)
  • As you visit/remove stops, the next ones from the queue
    automatically fill in
  • Far-away stops (like Eagle Lodge 3.6 mi from center) appear at
    the bottom of the queue, so they come last
  • Tap any route to load it instantly, then add/remove stops as needed
  • No manual setup needed


VISITS & ROUTES
---------------
When you mark a business as visited (from its map window or detail card),
it is automatically removed from your current route. The next stop from
the queue takes its place. This way your active route stays at ~15 stops
and you don't need to manually clean up visited stops.


FOLLOW-UPS & ROUTES
-------------------
In the Notes tab (including the ⏰ Follow-ups view), each business has
a "+ Add to Route" button. Tap it to add that business back to your
current route. This is useful when a follow-up date comes due:
  • Visit a business → it's removed from your route
  • Set a follow-up date on its note ("call back in 2 weeks")
  • When the date rolls around, the business shows up in ⏰ Follow-ups
  • Tap "+ Add to Route" to bring it back into your day's plan


SAVED ROUTES
------------
  • 💾 Save Current — name and save your route
  • Saved routes stay on your device
  • Tap a saved route to bring it back
  • ⬇ Export / ⬆ Import — make a backup file or load one


MAKING A SPREADSHEET
--------------------
Tap 📥 CSV at the top and pick a region:
  • Presque Isle Area
  • Houlton Region
  • Millinocket Area
  • Lincoln Area

A file will download with every business in that region and all their
information. Open it in Excel, Google Sheets, or Numbers.


RECENTLY VIEWED (🕐 Recent tab)
-------------------------------
Shows the last 10 businesses you looked at, newest first.
Tap one to go back to its details.


RESET
-----
Tap ↺ Reset to clear all your search choices and the current route.


LOSING INTERNET
---------------
If your internet connection goes out, a red bar appears at the top:
  "You are offline — map tiles may not load"
The map may not look right, but your notes, visits, and routes are
still there because they are saved on your device.


YOUR SAVED INFORMATION
----------------------
Notes, visits, routes, and your search choices are all saved on your
device. They stay there even if you close the page and come back.

To clear everything: go to your phone or computer settings and clear
site data for this page. Make an export backup first if you want to
keep anything.


TIPS FOR AD-SALES FIELD USE
---------------------------
  • Before you head out, pick one Town and review the list
  • Download the file for that region as a backup you can print
  • Mark visits with ✅ as you finish each call
  • Set a follow-up date when a business asks you to call back
  • Check ⏰ Follow-ups each morning for today's callbacks
  • Use the Route Planner to save driving time between stops
  • 📍 Navigate opens turn-by-turn directions on your phone
