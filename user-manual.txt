Coffee News of Aroostook — Business Directory Map
User Manual
===================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
An interactive map of 1,141 businesses across Aroostook County, Maine,
organized into 4 regions. Designed for ad-sales field use — browse, filter,
take notes, mark visits, plan routes, and export territory lists.

Open index.html in any browser. No server required.

BROWSING BUSINESSES
-------------------
On first load, the map shows all 863 businesses suitable for ads.
Markers are color-coded by category (see legend, bottom-right).

Tap any marker to see a popup with:
  • Business name, address, phone, email, website
  • Add/Remove from Route button
  • Directions (Google Maps)
  • Share button
  • Visit toggle


FILTERING
---------
Use the header controls to narrow results:

  Region ▼    — pick one of 4 regions
  Town ▼      — filtered by selected region
  Category ▼  — one of 7 category groups
  Search      — matches name, address, town, category, or phone digits
  🎤          — voice search (microphone icon)

Filter count badges (N) show how many businesses match each option.

Quick-filter pills (below header):
  All · Food · Shopping · Health · Auto · Services · Lodging · Religion
Tap one to instantly filter by business type. Count badges show results.


BUSINESS DETAIL PANE
--------------------
Tap a marker or tap a row in the Browse list to open the detail pane.
Shows full contact info with 📋 copy buttons for address and phone.

From the detail pane you can:
  • Get directions
  • Add/Remove from route
  • Share
  • Write a note
  • Set a follow-up date
  • Mark as visited
  • See nearby businesses (within 0.5 miles)


NOTES
-----
Every business can have a note (textarea) saved to your browser.
Access notes from:
  1. Detail pane — textarea with auto-save
  2. Notes & Visits sidebar tab

Each note can have a follow-up date (date picker).
When you save a note, the follow-up date is saved with it.

NOTES TAB (sidebar → 📝 Notes & Visits)
----------------------------------------
Tabs within the Notes tab:

  All           — businesses that have a note OR a visit
  With Notes    — only businesses that have a note
  Visited       — only businesses marked as visited
  + Add Note    — search any business to add a note
  ⏰ Follow-ups — grouped by urgency (see below)

Each business has:
  • Follow-up: <date input> — pick a callback date
  • Textarea — write the note
  • Save / Delete buttons
  • 🗺 Show on Map — center the map on this business
  • ✕ Remove — remove the last visit date

FOLLOW-UPS ⏰
-------------
The Follow-ups view groups businesses with follow-up dates into:

  🔴 Overdue (N)     — past due dates (red tags)
  🟠 Due Today (N)   — due today (orange tags)
  🔵 Coming Up (N)   — within 3 days (blue tags)
  📅 Later (N)       — more than 3 days out

The earliest follow-ups appear first within each section.


VISITS
------
Mark a business as visited from its popup or detail pane.
Each visit is date-stamped.
Visits show a green ✅ tag in the Notes tab.

To remove a visit: tap ✕ Remove next to the visit date.


ROUTE PLANNER (📍 Route)
------------------------
1. Tap markers on the map (or use detail pane) to add stops
2. Stops appear in the Route tab sidebar
3. Drag ⠿ handles to reorder
4. Tap 🗺 Optimize to reorder stops for shortest driving distance
   (uses OSRM routing engine — 2 retry attempts on failure)
5. Tap 📍 Navigate to open turn-by-turn in Apple Maps (iOS)
   or Google Maps (Android)
6. Tap 🖨 Print for a printed route sheet
7. Tap 🔊 Announce to hear stops read aloud (name + address)
8. Tap ✕ Clear to remove all stops

SAVED ROUTES
------------
  • 💾 Save Current — name and save the current route
  • Saved routes persist in your browser
  • Tap a saved route to restore it
  • ⬇ Export / ⬆ Import — backup or transfer routes as JSON


CSV EXPORT
----------
Tap 📥 CSV in the header → choose a region:
  • Presque Isle Area
  • Houlton Region
  • Millinocket Area
  • Lincoln Area

Downloads a UTF-8 CSV file with all 11 fields for every business
in that region. Open in Excel, Google Sheets, or Numbers.


RECENTLY VIEWED (🕐 Recent tab)
-------------------------------
Shows the last 10 businesses you viewed, newest first.
Tap one to jump back to its detail pane.


RESET
-----
Tap ↺ Reset to clear all filters, quick-filter selection, and
the current route.


OFFLINE
-------
If your internet connection drops, a red banner appears:
  "You are offline — map tiles may not load"
The app still works — cached tiles and all localStorage data
(notes, visits, routes) remain available.


STORAGE
-------
All notes, visits, saved routes, and filter preferences are stored
in your browser's localStorage. They persist across page reloads.
To clear them: browser settings → Clear site data.

Export notes/visits/routes as JSON for backup before clearing.


TIPS FOR AD-SALES FIELD USE
----------------------------
  • Before heading out, filter to one Town and review the list
  • Download the CSV for that region as a printed backup
  • Mark visits with ✅ as you complete calls
  • Set a follow-up date when a business asks you to call back
  • Check ⏰ Follow-ups each morning for today's callbacks
  • Use Route Planner to optimize your driving route between stops
  • 📍 Navigate opens turn-by-turn directions on your phone
